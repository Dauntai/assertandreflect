---

Fast feedback is the antidote to fear. When tests return results quickly and with clear context, teams respond faster and ship with more confidence.

Make the trade-offs explicit: what to run on each push, what to reserve for nightly, and how to make failures actionable.

Small improvements — a 30-second smoke, meaningful logs, and deterministic tests — compound into much faster resolution times.
